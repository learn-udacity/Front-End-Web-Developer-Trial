03. Final Tips

You now have all the information you need to proceed on your Nanodegree journey! Some final tips to help you get off to a running start:

  • Download Udacity's mobile app to learn on the go!

  • Check out the Resources tab (on the left of the page) to access these resources and more. You'll find resources next to every project you do in your Nanodegree path. Our mission is to make your learning experience as effective and smooth as possible.

  • Remember to put in time consistently, engage with your community (Study Group, Knowledge, etc.), take advantage of the resources available to you (especially your Career Portal, also accessible via the briefcase icon in the left panel of your classroom), and give us feedback throughout the program.

  • Use your calendar! One great way to set aside time to work on your Nanodegree program is to create a repeating event in a calendar app such as Google Calendar. You can have it remind you on your phone or computer when it's time to study. We recommend setting aside ten hours a week, in two or more blocks of time. This might mean two hours on each weekday evening, or three or four hours three times a week, or whatever fits your schedule. But it's really important to actually set this time aside. Take a moment to do that right now while you're thinking about it.

We are so glad to have you with us!
