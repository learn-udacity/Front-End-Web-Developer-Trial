Connecting with your community

We've created a community on Slack, an awesome chat tool that allows you to reach out to your Nanodegree community anytime!

Slack is a place you can chat with current students and graduates of this program to get help on your projects, share great blog posts and new discoveries, and generally hangout. Join the team now!

You can head to the forum to ask and answer questions and contribute to discussions. Engage with other students and answer each other's questions! You can search to see if someone has already asked a question related to yours, or you can make a new post if no one has.
